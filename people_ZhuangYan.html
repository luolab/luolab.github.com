Technician

ZHUANG Yan

(2011-present)


Email: z75y20@163.com


Research Interest: Molecular Biology

 

After receiving his B.S. in biology from Ocean University of China in 2000, ZHUANG Yan has been working as a laboratory technician at various institutions for over twelve years. He is a master in tackling regular or challenging technical issues in molecular and cellular biology experiments.  Yan joined us in 2011 and is in charge of the lab’s general supply ordering and overall maintenance.  He is also playing important roles in various projects in the lab ranging from tiger ancient DNA to bear molecular scatology.
