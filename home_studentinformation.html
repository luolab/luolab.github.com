Interested in joining the Luo Laboratory?

Undergraduates
Whether you enjoy going out to the field, pipetting in the lab, or sitting in front of the computer for analysis, we have various opportunities for undergraduate students to join the lab.  If you are interested in evolution, biogeography, population genetics, genomics, ecology, or wildlife conservation, detail-oriented, disciplined, and motivated, please contact Dr. Luo at Luo.shujin@pku.edu.cn, to discuss your research interests.

Graduate Students
We always have an eye out for highly motivated, detail-oriented, and dedicated graduate students with interests in evolution, biogeography, population genetics, genomics, ecology, or wildlife conservation.  There are three ways we recruited graduate students:

(1) Graduate Program at the School of Life Sciences at PKU (SLS-PKU);

(2) Peking-Tsinghua-NIBS Graduate Program (PTN);

(3) Peking-Tsinghua Center for Life Sciences (CLS).

More detailed information about the graduate programs and the admissions process can be found at: SLS-PKU and CLS. If you are interested, please contact Dr. Luo first via email at Luo.shujin@pku.edu.cn to discuss about the opportunities.

 

Postdoctoral Researchers
Please contact Dr. Luo directly via email at Luo.shujin@pku.edu.cn for information about research opportunities that are available in the lab.  Internationally competitive postdoctoral fellowships are available for strong candidates through the Peking-Tsinghua Center for Life Sciences (CLS)

Other Opportunities
We have a tradition to welcome a diverse array of visitors to work in the lab, ranging from high school students to senior scientists.  Please contact Dr. Luo directly via email at Luo.shujin@pku.edu.cn for information about short- or long-term visiting and training opportunities.
