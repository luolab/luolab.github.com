Technician

TIAN Yihua (2011-present)
Email: tyh-12282001@163.com

 

 

 

 

 

TIAN Yihua has over twelve years’ experience of lab technician.  She worked at the Peking University Health Science Center from 2000 to 2011, where she has become familiar with the general techniques in molecular biology, biochemistry and immunology.  Yihua joined us since 2011 and currently serves as the administrative associate of the lab and assistance to the PI.  She is also providing molecular laboratory assistance whenever necessary.
