Research interest: Evolution & cats                                          

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Tian Yijie is an undergraduate student at the School of Life Sciences, Peking University since 2014. She is interested in evolutionary sciences, with a particular interest in the color variation and behavior of cats. Growing up in Sichuan, a province where countless pristine mountains and clear rivers still exist, she loves nature and wildlife. She is now participating in a project concerning the color variation of the spotted seal (Phoca largha).
